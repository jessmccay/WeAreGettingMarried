So you're going to Montana?

There are a lot of great things to do and see!

Glacier National Park

Take a day trip or go camping surrounding by breathtaking beauty. You havent quite lived if you havent ridden down The Sun Road! Learn more about Glacier National Park Here

Yellowstone National Park

If you are making the trek to Montana, why not travel to the famous Yellowstone National Park? Learn more about Yellowstone National Park Here

Garden of 1000 Buddha's

Just down the road in Arlee you will find the Garden of 1000 Buddha's. If you want a place of zen to practice meditation or maybe you are just interested in seeing this awesome site. It is well worth your time and it's free! Learn more about Garden of 1000 Buddha's Here

Flathead Lake

Love the water? Well then you must take a dip in the largest natural freshwater lake west of the Mississippi! Learn more about Flathead Lake Here

The 'M'

Hike the Missoula 'M'. See the beautiful view of Missoula from above while getting to breath that fresh Montana air.

Missoula Farmers Market

On Saturday's downtown missoula hosts the missoula farmer's market where you can find delicious breakfast food, fresh produce and some very neat hand made goods.
