So, you're going to Montana?

There are a lot of great things to do and see!

National Bison Range

You must visit the National Bison Range! It is just down the road and such an incredible experience. Learn more about the Bison Range here.

Flathead Lake

Love the water? Well then you must take a dip in the largest natural freshwater lake west of the Mississippi! Learn more about Flathead Lake here.

The 'M'

Hike the Missoula 'M'. See the beautiful view of Missoula from above while getting to breath that fresh Montana air.

Arlee Celebration

Arlee is a unique little place. This is the Flathead Indian Reservation. During this time of year it will be the 120th Arlee celebration. Hear the sound of the dancer's jingle dress, see the beautiful homemade traditional Native American robes, witness a stickgame match and so much more. Learn more about your Native brothers and sisters and visit the powwow! Click here to learn more.

Glacier National Park

Take a day trip or go camping and be surrounded by breathtaking beauty. You havent quite lived until riding down The Sun Road! Wildlife sightings are likely. Learn more about Glacier here.

Garden of 1000 Buddha's

Just down the road in Arlee you will find the Garden of 1000 Buddha's. This impressive site is free to visit.Learn more about Garden of 1000 Buddha's here.

Missoula Farmers Market

On Saturday mornings, downtown missoula hosts the missoula farmer's market where you can find delicious breakfast food, fresh produce and some very neat hand made goods.

Yellowstone National Park

If you are making the trek to Montana, why not travel to the famous Yellowstone National Park? Learn more about Yellowstone National Park here.
